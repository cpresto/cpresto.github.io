Fireworks

ABOUT THIS PICTURE

This picture was taken on the Norcross, GA 3rd of July Fireworks show. In order to capture fireworks using a camera, you need to slow down the shutter speed and use a tripod. I took several shots using 2-3 second shutter speeds.
